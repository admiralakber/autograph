AUTOGRAPH — the manual

These are lifeforms trying to draw their true self out of the false. You're lending compute to the search. What you're watching is real evolution; where something is illustrative rather than proven, we say so.

The strange loop

Each creature is a tiny neural network. Its DNA is a compositional pattern-producing network (a CPPN). In HyperNEAT the DNA paints the weights of a second network — the phenotype, the brain — and (simplified ES-HyperNEAT) it also places that brain's neurons. The phenotype, read over 3D space, draws a volumetric self-portrait. Read the self-portrait back at known points and you recover a DNA′. The closer DNA′ is to the DNA, the more the loop has closed — Escher's Drawing Hands, alive. Loop fidelity is measured live and never faked.

Render = network = code

The single most misunderstood thing: a beautiful render is a neural network, and that network has a DNA. Toggle SELF-PORTRAIT / PHENOTYPE / DNA on the same individual to see one thing three ways. That is genotype → phenotype → render, shown rather than lectured.

You, and the swarm

Today this runs entirely on your device — a single local node, no backend, no telemetry. The roadmap is a shared world: many machines growing one genealogy, coordinated by a sandboxed PartyServer coordinator. The signed tree of life persists in your browser and grows across visits; one day it becomes everyone's.

What's real vs illustrative

  • Real: the evolving CPPN + HyperNEAT substrate, simplified ES-HyperNEAT placement, the 3D volumetric render, MAP-Elites quality-diversity, loop fidelity (measured), and a signed, hash-chained lineage persisted in IndexedDB.
  • Illustrative / roadmap: the worldwide swarm (single device for now), zk "proof of becoming", full quadtree ES-HyperNEAT (we ship a simplified placement), and the quantum framing — metaphor and lineage only. There are no qubits here.
